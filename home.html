Features

OPEN SOURCE

WhisperVC is an open-source project that provides a secure and private voice and video communication platform. The software's source code is openly available, and with the help of the project's documentation, interested parties can build and customize their own version of the software according to their needs.

CUSTOMIZATION

WhisperVC will offer extensive customization options, allowing users to modify the platform to meet their specific needs. This customization will be made possible through the software's open-source nature and thorough documentation, which will provide users with step-by-step guidance on how to customize the platform to suit their unique requirements.
